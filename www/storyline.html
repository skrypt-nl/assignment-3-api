Storyline

  • In the near future, Earth is facing an agricultural crisis and a global famine.

  • Cooper, a former NASA pilot and engineer, is now a farmer living with his children, Tom and Murph.

  • Murph discovers a mysterious pattern in her room that leads Cooper and her to a secret NASA facility.

  • There, they learn that NASA has been working on a plan to save humanity by finding a new habitable planet through a wormhole near Saturn.

  • Cooper is recruited to pilot the mission along with a team of scientists, including Dr. Brand, the daughter of Professor Brand, the mastermind behind the mission.

  • Cooper and his team travel through the wormhole and encounter several promising planets, but also face various challenges and setbacks, including time dilation.

  • They eventually land on a planet near a black hole, where they spend several years searching for a way to escape the planet's gravitational pull and continue their mission.

  • While on the planet, Cooper receives a transmission from his daughter Murph, who is now an adult and working with Professor Brand on Earth.

  • Murph informs Cooper that Plan A, which involved solving the gravitational equations to launch humanity into space, has failed, and that Plan B, involving the use of frozen human embryos to repopulate a new planet, is the only option left.

  • Cooper decides to return to Earth and sends Dr. Brand's daughter, Amelia, to continue the mission alone.

  • Cooper ends up in a tesseract created by future humans where he can see and interact with events from different moments in time, including his past and future selves.

  • Cooper communicates with Murph through the tesseract and gives her the information she needs to solve the gravitational equation, which she uses to launch the remaining humans into space.

  • The mission is successful, and the remaining humans settle on a new habitable planet.
