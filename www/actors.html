Actors

Discover the talented ensemble cast that brought the story of Interstellar to life on the big screen.

Dr. Cooper

Matthew McConaughey

Dr. Cooper is a determined astronaut chosen to lead a mission to save humanity. He uses his skills and love for his family to navigate through the challenges of time and space.

Murph

Mackenzie Foy

Murph is the daughter of Dr. Cooper. She is a bright and curious child with a strong intuition who plays a crucial role in the success of the mission to save humanity.

Tom

Casey Affleck

Cooper's son, who stays on Earth to work on his family's farm while his father is away on the mission.

T.A.R.S.

Bill Irwin

T.A.R.S is the sarcastic and witty robot aboard the spaceship in "Interstellar". He provides much-needed comic relief and assists the crew on their mission to save humanity.

Brand

Anna Hathaway

A scientist and astronaut who accompanies Cooper on the mission to find a new planet. She is the daughter of the lead scientist on the project, Professor Brand.

Professor Brand

Michael Caine

The lead scientist on the project to save humanity. He is dying, but continues to work on the project until the end.
