Awards

"Interstellar" received critical acclaim, won several awards, and was nominated for many others. It won the Academy Award for Best Visual Effects, British Academy Film Award for Best Special Visual Effects, and Saturn Award for Best Science Fiction Film. It was also nominated for a Golden Globe Award. The film is praised for its storytelling, visuals, and performances. See the table below for a summary of its awards.
